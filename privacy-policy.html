Last Updated: July 21, 2023

We may collect non-personal identification information about users whenever they interact with our Plugin. This may include the browser name, the type of computer, technical information about the user's means of connection to our Plugin, such as the operating system and the internet service providers utilized, and other similar information.

1.3. Web Search Data

The Plugin may collect information about the search queries that users make. This information is collected in an anonymized and aggregated manner.

We use the information we collect to understand user needs and preferences, improve our Plugin and develop new features. The information is also used to troubleshoot issues, understand usage trends, and improve the overall quality and effectiveness of our Plugin.
